CASA DEL SOL

Embrace the Finest in Villa Living

Unforgettable Experiences

Welcome to Casa del Sol, where sophistication meets sun-kissed bliss. This Mediterranean-inspired villa boasts spacious interiors, a private infinity pool, and panoramic vistas of golden sunsets over the horizon. With its impeccable attention to detail and a seamless blend of indoor and outdoor living, Casa del Sol is the epitome of Mediterranean luxury.

LIVING AREA

Upon entering the villa, you will be greeted by an atmosphere of elegance and sophistication. The spacious and tastefully decorated living area features plush sofas, exquisite artwork, and large windows that bathe the space in natural light while offering panoramic views of the surrounding beauty. The open-plan layout seamlessly connects the living area with the dining space, creating a sense of fluidity and an ideal setting for shared moments with loved ones.




KITCHEN

The fully equipped gourmet kitchen is a chef's dream, complete with top-of-the-line appliances, sleek cabinetry, and ample counter space. Whether you prefer to showcase your culinary skills or indulge in the services of a private chef, this kitchen provides everything you need to create and enjoy sumptuous meals.




MASTER SUITE

The master suite exudes luxury, boasting a king-size bed, premium linens, and a private balcony or terrace where you can savor the breathtaking vistas of the Amalfi Coast. The en-suite bathroom is a sanctuary of indulgence, adorned with exquisite fixtures, a soaking tub, and a separate rainfall shower.

SECOND ROOM

The second bedroom, equally inviting, offers twin beds and its own en-suite bathroom, ensuring privacy and comfort for all guests.

OUTSIDE AREA

Stepping outside, you'll discover a captivating outdoor area that epitomizes the allure of Amalfi. The villa is surrounded by lush gardens, fragrant lemon trees, and vibrant bougainvillea, creating a private oasis of serenity. A spacious terrace provides the perfect setting for relaxation and entertainment, featuring comfortable loungers, a dining area, and a shaded pergola where you can unwind while enjoying the gentle sea breeze.
